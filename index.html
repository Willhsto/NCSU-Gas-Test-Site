Cylinder rental: invoicing and inventory

↑ Back to top

NC State departments rent gas cylinders from the MarketPlace suppliers. Cylinder rental invoices are processed for the number of days the cylinder is on campus and are posted each month. Cylinder inventory reports are posted each month. To meet lab safety and financial compliance standards, follow these steps.

  1. 1

    Receive the order

    Confirm delivery and complete the receiving process correctly.

    How to Receive a MarketPlace Order
  2. 2

    Record each cylinder

    Record cylinder movements so inventory and billing stay aligned.

  3. 3

    Review invoice

    Open the invoice report to review monthly rental charges.

    Open invoice report
  4. 4

    Review inventory

    Check the current inventory report for cylinders on campus.

    Open inventory report

    Match inventory and invoice

    Reconcile inventory and invoice data to verify proper rental billing.
